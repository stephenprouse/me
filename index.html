About

Stephen is a nurse leader and informatics executive who connects clinical practice, digital transformation, governance, and operational improvement. His background spans emergency nursing, project leadership, clinical applications, enterprise applications, and executive informatics leadership.

Professional Summary

He has led teams responsible for planning, developing, testing, implementing, optimizing, and supporting clinical information systems across multi-hospital environments. His work consistently focuses on practical adoption, clinician experience, and measurable operational impact.

Stephen is especially known for leading enterprise clinical initiatives, reducing documentation burden, advancing e-consent strategy, supporting telehealth programs, and amplifying the voice of frontline staff in electronic medical record change processes.
